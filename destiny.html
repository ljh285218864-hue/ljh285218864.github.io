Destiny readings

Written readings for one or a few sincere questions: career timing, relationship patterns, or the deeper rhythm of the years ahead. Each reading is a reflection, not a prediction carved in stone.

Single-question reading

A focused written reading around one central question. You share the context, your current situation, and what you genuinely wish to understand. The reading explores timing, underlying patterns, and possible directions.

Three-question deep dive

For moments of transition. Three related questions are read together as one landscape: career, emotional life, and personal growth. The emphasis is on how different areas of life echo each other, rather than isolated problems.

How to begin

To begin, you can use the question form to share your name, birth details, and the question(s) that you are carrying right now.
