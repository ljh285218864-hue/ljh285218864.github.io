What is Taoist Destiny Reading?

什么是道家命理解读？

Taoist destiny reading uses your birth data and time cycles to understand patterns in your life, such as career, wealth, and relationships.

道家命理解读会结合你的出生信息与时间周期的变化，来了解你在事业、财运、感情等方面的人生命运模式。

It is not about controlling fate, but about making wiser choices with better awareness.

它并不是要控制命运，而是通过更清晰的认知，帮助你做出更明智的选择。
