FAQ

Answers to common questions

Are the sessions fully online?
Yes. All destiny reading and feng shui sessions are held online through a video call. You can join from anywhere in the U.S. as long as you have a stable internet connection.
What information do you need from me?
For destiny readings, I’ll need your birth date, time, and city. For feng shui consultations, I’ll ask for photos or a simple layout of your home or office. We can share those privately by email after your booking is confirmed.
How do payments work?
You can pay through the payment links on this page or after we confirm your booking by email. All payments are processed in USD through recognized platforms like Stripe or PayPal.
Do you ship outside the U.S. for products?
At the moment, physical products ship within the U.S. only. If you’re outside the U.S., you can still book online sessions.
Do you guarantee specific results?
I don’t guarantee specific outcomes. My role is to provide insight into timing, patterns, and options so you can make better-informed choices. Many clients find the sessions surprisingly accurate and helpful.
