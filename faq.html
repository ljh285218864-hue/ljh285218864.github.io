FAQ

常见问题

1. How do I book a reading?

1. 我如何预约命理解读？

You can choose a service, then contact us via WhatsApp / email to confirm details and receive your payment link.

你可以先选择想要的服务，然后通过 WhatsApp / 邮箱联系我，确认细节后会收到支付链接。


2. How long does it take to receive my reading?

2. 解读多久可以完成？

Depending on the service, most readings are delivered within 3–5 working days.

根据不同服务，一般会在 3–5 个工作日内完成解读。


3. Do you offer refunds?

3. 可以退款吗？

For physical products, we can discuss returns if there are quality issues. For readings, once the analysis is delivered, usually no refund is provided.

实物产品如有质量问题可以协商退换；命理解读一旦已经完成并交付，通常不支持退款。


4. What information do you need from me?

4. 你需要我提供哪些信息？

For destiny readings, we usually need your birth date, time, place, and main questions you care about.

命理解读通常需要你的出生日期、时间、地点，以及你最关心的几个问题。


5. How do you protect my privacy?

5. 我的隐私如何保护？

All readings are 1:1 and private. We do not share your personal data with anyone.

所有解读都是一对一私密进行，不会对外泄露你的个人资料。
