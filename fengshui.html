FENG SHUI

Feng Shui notes

Practical, gentle suggestions for your room, working desk, or altar space. The aim is to support your daily rhythm, not to turn your home into a rigid project.

Room and sleeping area

We look at basic directions, light, and where your bed is placed in relation to doors and windows. Adjustments are suggested with respect for your existing furniture and constraints.

Desk and work corner

Small changes to where you sit, what you face, and how symbolic items are placed can shift the feeling of your working hours, especially if you spend many hours at a screen.

Altar or quiet corner

For those who wish to maintain a small altar, suggestions focus on proportion, simplicity, and the emotional meaning of each object rather than complexity.
