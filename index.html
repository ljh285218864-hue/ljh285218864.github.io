FOUNDER'S NOTE

Reading time and place

In Taoist-inspired destiny work, time and place are never separate. Your birth data describes a starting rhythm, but your current environment reshapes that rhythm every day.

A reading looks at how your question sits inside this meeting point between what you were born with and where you are now: your work, your relationships, your city, your habits.

ON LIVING WITH DESTINY

From insight to action

Many people come to destiny readings when something is about to change: a job, a relationship, a place to live. Insight is important, but it only becomes real when it shapes your next steps.

This is why each reading includes both symbolic language and very practical suggestions: what to stop feeding, what to commit to, and what deserves more patience.
