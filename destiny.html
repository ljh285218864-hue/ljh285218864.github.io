Reading types
Main options

Below is a simple framework for the main types of readings. You can adjust the names and prices later, but the structure is ready.

Career · Work

Career & direction reading

For questions about changing jobs, long-term work direction, or how to use your strengths more clearly in your current environment.

Marriage · Relationship

Marriage & relationship reading

For questions about long-term partnerships, repeating patterns in relationships, and emotional needs that keep showing up.

Health · Rhythm

Health & life rhythm reading

Not a medical reading. Focused on how your habits, stress, and timing interact with your body’s needs, from a Taoist-inspired perspective.

Long-term themes

Destiny overview reading

A broad look at long-term tendencies: environments that support you, how you respond to change, and where you tend to feel most alive.

Order framework
Consultation and payment steps

This framework is ready for you to use. Later you can plug in your real prices and payment links. For now, it shows the complete flow from choosing a reading to sending details.

  1. Choose your reading type – career, relationship, health rhythm, or destiny overview.
  2. Confirm price and payment method – for example, PayPal or another service.
  3. Fill in the question form – on the question.html page.
  4. Receive confirmation – you get an email confirming your request and expected delivery time.
  5. Receive the written reading – within the agreed time frame.
