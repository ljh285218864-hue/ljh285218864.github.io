Process
How a reading usually works

The basic flow is the same for most readings. Details may be slightly different depending on what you choose, but the intention stays simple and respectful.

Step 1

Choose a focus

You decide whether you want to focus on career, relationship, health rhythm, or a broader destiny overview.

Step 2

Send your details

After payment, you share your name, date of birth, main question, and any context needed to understand your situation.

Step 3

Receive your reading

You receive a written reading in English within a few working days. You can re-read it slowly and mark what resonates.

Step 4

Optional follow-up

Short clarifying questions may be possible. Deeper or new topics are usually handled as a new reading.
