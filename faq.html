FAQ

Answers to the most common questions before you place an order.

1. What information do you need for a reading?

For most readings, you will be asked for your name or nickname, your date of birth, and your main question.

If a specific method requires more details, that will be clearly explained before you confirm your order.

2. How long does it take to receive my reading?

Most readings are delivered within 3–5 working days after we receive your details and question.

During busy periods or holidays, delivery may take a little longer, but we will inform you if that happens.

3. In what format will I receive the reading?

You will receive your reading as a written text in English, which can be sent by email or a messaging platform, depending on your preference.

Written format allows you to re-read and reflect at your own pace.

4. Do readings or products guarantee results?

No. Both readings and products are tools for reflection and intention-setting.

Real change depends on your decisions, actions, and support from appropriate professionals where needed.

5. Can I ask follow-up questions after a reading?

Short clarifications may be possible, but extensive follow-up is usually handled through a new reading request.

This keeps the process clear and fair for both sides.

6. Are there any situations where I should not rely on a reading?

Yes. If you are facing a crisis related to physical or mental health, legal trouble, or serious financial risk, please contact qualified professionals or local emergency services first.

Readings are not a replacement for urgent or professional help.
