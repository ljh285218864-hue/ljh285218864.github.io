DESTINY

Destiny readings written with care.

Readings focus on one or a few sincere questions: timing for career decisions, emotional patterns in relationships, or the overall rhythm of coming years. The writing combines Taoist-style observation with your own story.

Each reading is delivered as a written document so you can revisit it quietly, in your own time and space.

FENG SHUI

Feng Shui as quiet support.

Rather than perfection, Feng Shui here is approached as a series of calm adjustments: the placement of a desk, the angle of a bed, or a small altar that marks your intention without overwhelming your life.

Suggestions are realistic and gentle, designed to work with your actual space, schedule, and resources.
